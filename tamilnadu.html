Scholarship To Son & Daughter Of Differently Abled Person

The financial amount will be provided to those children whose one of their earning parents is differently-abled and are not be able to pay the fees of their children. The main objective of launching this scholarship is to provide them with a better future and learning opportunity

Know More About It

  • The student of classes 1 to 5 will be rewarded with a scholarship of Rs.
  • Rs. 3,000 per annum for three years

R. I. M. C Dehradun Scholarship

The Government of Tamilnadu has launched is a scheme in order to provide educational facilities to the young men of the state belonging to the military as well as civil. The main purpose of this scholarship is to provide an opportunity to the student to become lawyers, engineers, corporate leaders, captains of industries, etc..

Know More About It

  • INR 500 per annum to students of class 3rd to 5th INR 1,000 per annum to students of class 6th
  • National Talent Search Exam NTSE

Deceased Government Servants Scholarship

The benefit of this scholarship will be provided to the student whose parent has been a disease in doing the government job. The main aim of launching this scheme is to help them with the educational facilities after the death of their parents.

Know More About It

  • Rs. 3,000 per annum for four years or till the normal completion of the course
  • Up to Rs. 6,000 per annum for class 11th & 12th

EVR Nagammai Scholarship

This scholarship will be provided to those students who are currently pursuing their post-graduation courses in various streams like arts commerce and science . Only those students are eligible who are permanent residents of Tamilnadu

Know More About It

  • Examination fees, special fees, and other compulsory fees up to Rs. 10,000 (approximate)
  • Financial assistance of up to Rs. 7,000 per annum

Free Education For BC MBC And DNC

The Government of Tamilnadu has created this scheme in order to provide financial help to those students who belong to BC MBC and DNC category. Also, the students who are pursuing a three-year degree or polytechnic or professional diploma courses are eligible to apply.

Know More About It

  • Tuition fees, hostel fees, and other admissible items of special fees up to UG level
  • Financial assistance of up to Rs. 6,000
