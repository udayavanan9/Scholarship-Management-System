About Us

UD Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled. UD Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan (NeGP)

Scholarship Details

The Mission Mode Project (MMP) of UD Scholarships Portal under the National e-Governance Plan aims at providing common electronic portal for implementing various Scholarships schemes launched by Union Government, State Government and Union Territories across the country.
